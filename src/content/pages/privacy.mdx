# Privacy Policy

**Quillstash** is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information.

## 1. Data Collection

- We do not collect any personal information beyond what is required for account creation (e.g., username, email address).
- We do not track your activities or behavior on **Quillstash**.

## 2. Use of Information

- Your information will never be shared with third parties.
- We do not use your information for marketing purposes or to create user profiles.

## 3. Cookies

- **Quillstash** does not use cookies or similar technologies to track users.

## 4. Account Deletion

- You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. Once deleted, your data will not be recoverable.

## 5. Security

- We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, but we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

- We may update these terms and policies occasionally. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Terms of Service or Privacy Policy, please contact us at [support@quillstash.com](mailto:support@quillstash.com).
